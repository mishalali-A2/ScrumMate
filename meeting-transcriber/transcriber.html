How to Use

  1. Copy your Google Meet URL
  2. Paste the URL and click "Join Meeting"
  3. The bot will join and start transcribing in real-time
  4. When done, click "Leave Meeting" to save the transcript
  5. Click "Run Pipeline" to generate meeting minutes, user stories, and assignments
