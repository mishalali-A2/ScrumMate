Before you join

  1. Paste a full Google Meet URL.
  2. Pick a meeting type (it drives the pipeline for minutes and stories).
  3. Press Join — when the bot enters the call, you go to the live transcript page.
  4. Keep this tab open while recording.

After the call

  1. On the transcript page, Leave stops the bot and saves the file.
  2. Use Run pipeline in the save banner for summaries and assignments.
  3. AI Query searches past meetings.
